Our Story

The heart behind every hamper

Hi, I'm Khosi Mhayise — the heart behind Aesthetic Gifts, based in Mkhondo, Mpumalanga.

It all began in my kitchen, wrapping small gifts for friends and family. Today, I pour that same love into every hamper, believing that gifting is more than just giving an item — it's about sharing love, joy, and creating lasting memories.

Every hamper is handcrafted with care, elegance, and attention to detail, tailored for birthdays, weddings, baby showers, and corporate occasions.

When you choose us, you're not just buying a gift — you're sharing a heartfelt moment.
